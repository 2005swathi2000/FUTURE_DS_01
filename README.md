📊 FUTURE_DS_01 – Retail Sales Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover business insights such as revenue trends, top-performing product categories, and regional sales performance. The analysis helps businesses understand customer purchasing behavior and make data-driven decisions to improve sales performance.

🎯 Objective

Analyze business sales data to identify:

Revenue trends over time

Top-selling product categories

Regional sales performance

High-value product segments

The goal is to generate actionable insights for business growth and strategic decision-making.

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Python

Jupyter Notebook

Pandas – Data manipulation and analysis

Matplotlib – Data visualization

Seaborn – Advanced data visualization

📊 Key Analysis Performed
1️⃣ Sales by Region

Analyzed total sales across different regions to identify high-performing markets.

2️⃣ Sales by Product Category

Identified the most profitable product categories contributing to revenue.

3️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend

Studied monthly sales patterns to understand revenue growth and seasonal trends.

4️⃣ Region vs Category Heatmap

Created a heatmap to visualize sales distribution across regions and product categories.

📈 Key Insights

Certain regions generate significantly higher sales, indicating strong market demand.

Product category performance varies by region, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing.

Sales trends show seasonal fluctuations, highlighting peak sales periods.

Some categories contribute a larger share of overall revenue, making them strategic focus areas.

💡 Business Recommendations

Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions to maximize revenue.

Promote top-selling product categories to increase overall profitability.

Optimize inventory based on seasonal demand patterns.

Explore expansion opportunities in regions with growing sales trends.
